Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Go to Orders tab and tap on any active order to see real-time tracking with map view and estimated delivery time.

How can I cancel my order?

You can cancel within 60 seconds of placing the order from the tracking screen. After that, contact support for assistance.

How do refunds work?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method. Wallet refunds are instant.

How to apply a coupon code?

On the cart screen, enter your coupon code in the "Enter coupon code" field and tap APPLY. Valid coupons will automatically discount your order.

Can I change my delivery address?

You can change the address before placing the order on the cart screen. After placing, contact support for address changes.
